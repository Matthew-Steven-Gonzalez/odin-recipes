Lasagna Recipe

A famous italian dish made up of layers of cheese, meats, noodles, and other popular ingredients.

Also famous for being hated by a famous orange cat

ingredients

  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Itilian Sausage
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Mozzerella Cheese
  • Tomato Sauce

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees and beging boiling water to soften noodles.
  2. Once noodles are soft begin layering cheese mix, tomato sauce, and cooked sausage.
  3. Cook for 3 hours or until cheese is fully melted.

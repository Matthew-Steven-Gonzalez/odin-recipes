Enchilada Recipe

What better way to end the day than with a big old batch of enchiladas? There's no doubt that enchiladas are one of the most popular items on the menu at Mexican restaurants, and the age-old question is always which do you prefer â€” chicken or beef? This particular recipe calls for a delicious beef and onion mixture paired with your traditional enchilada sauce, a flavorful salsa and sour cream mix, and of course, Mexican cheese that melts perfectly over the top of the tortilla.

ingredients

  • Corn Torillas
  • Oxaca Cheese
  • Red/Green sauce
  • Hamburger Meat
  • Taco Seasoning Blend

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
  2. Cook meat with taco seasoning and begin to lightly fry all tortillas.
  3. Then you will roll in the tortilla a blend of meat and cheese.
  4. Once rollled layer in a baking dish and cover with sauce and cheese.
  5. Cook for 1 hours or until cheese is fully melted.
